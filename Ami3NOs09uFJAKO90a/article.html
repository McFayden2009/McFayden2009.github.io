TOURISTS BARRED ENTRY TO PARIS CATACOMBS FOLLOWING VIRAL OUTBREAK

empty paris catacombs during pandemic

Tourists to Paris aiming to visit the catacombs have been shunned at the door following reports of flu-like symptoms among employees and visitors, with city officials and medical experts authorising a quarantine of the attraction after examination of the afflicted proved that the virus was not the flu, nor any other common virus.

Some experts propose that the illness might be a new strain of covid-19, with radical groups around the worlds marking it as a sign of an oncoming apocalypse, although Pierre Lagarde, head of the Pariah medical institute in Bordeaux condemns the doomsaying of these religious groups as "Blatant fearmongering perpetrated by fanatics with archaic belief systems."

-Emilis Kavaliova, Sunset Times - 5th march 2024
